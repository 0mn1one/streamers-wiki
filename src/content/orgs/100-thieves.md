**100 Thieves** is a gaming, esports, and lifestyle brand founded in 2017 by former Call of Duty world champion Matthew "Nadeshot" Haag. The organization operates:

- Esports teams in Valorant, League of Legends, and other titles.
- A content creator roster including Valkyrae (who became a co-owner in 2021), CouRage, BrookeAB, Jack "CouRage" Dunlop and others.
- An apparel line with frequent drop-culture releases and collaborations (Juicy Couture, Gucci).
- A Los Angeles compound and studio space.

The organization raised from investors including Drake, Scooter Braun, and Dan Gilbert.

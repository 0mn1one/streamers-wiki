**KOI** is a Spanish esports organization co-founded by Ibai Llanos and Gerard Piqué in April 2022. The org operates competitive teams across League of Legends, Valorant, and other titles, and produces Ibai's annual **La Velada del Año** stadium boxing event. In 2023, KOI merged with Rogue's LEC slot, placing it in Europe's top-tier League of Legends competition.

**Hololive Production** is a VTuber talent agency operated by Cover Corp (東証グロース: 5253). Founded in 2016, it has grown into the dominant global VTuber agency, operating branch rosters across Japanese (Hololive JP), English (Hololive EN / Myth / Council / Advent / Justice), Indonesian (Hololive ID), and previously Chinese and Mexican markets. Cover Corp went public on the Tokyo Stock Exchange in 2023. Notable Hololive talents include Gawr Gura, Usada Pekora, Mori Calliope, Watson Amelia, Houshou Marine, and dozens of others.

**AMP** ("Any Means Possible") is a creator collective co-founded by Kai Cenat, Fanum, Duke Dennis, Agent 00, ImDavisss and Chrisnxtdoor in 2019. The group's YouTube channel features challenge and sketch content; individually its members comprise a significant share of Twitch's top-tier Just Chatting streamers. AMP is one of the defining collectives of the post-2020 Twitch era alongside OTK and Offline TV.

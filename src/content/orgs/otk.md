**OTK** (One True King) is a streamer-owned creator collective founded in January 2021 by Mizkif, Asmongold, Esfand, Rich Campbell, Tips Out, Mitch Jones, Sodapoppin, CDawgVA, Emiru, Nmplol and Malena. OTK has grown from a content org into a full media company running event productions, management, merchandise, and the **Streamer Awards**, an annual creator-community awards show launched in 2022.

The organization has been at the center of several high-profile internal disputes, most notably the 2022 controversy that led to Mizkif temporarily stepping away from the organization.

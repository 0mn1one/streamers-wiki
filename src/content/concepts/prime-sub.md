A **Prime sub** is a free monthly Tier 1 Twitch subscription granted to every Amazon Prime member. Viewers can redeem one Prime sub per month to any streamer of their choice. The streamer receives payment (at a reduced per-sub rate vs. a paid Tier 1) and a sub counts toward Twitch milestones. Prime subs are often the first engagement path from casual viewer to subscriber and typically make up a meaningful share of any Partner's monthly sub count.

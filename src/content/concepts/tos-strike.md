## Overview

A **TOS strike** (or simply "strike") is a suspension issued when a streamer violates a platform's Terms of Service. On Twitch, common durations are 24 hours, 3 days, 7 days, 30 days, or indefinite. Repeat offenses typically escalate. Platforms rarely publish detailed strike reasons publicly, leading to speculation in chat and on social media.

## Common triggers

- Nudity or sexual content
- Hateful conduct / harassment
- Copyrighted music (also handled via DMCA)
- Gambling on unauthorized sites (since Twitch's 2022 restrictions)
- Dangerous behavior / threats
- IRL bystander exposure

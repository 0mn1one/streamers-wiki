**NPC streaming** is a TikTok Live performance style popularized in 2023, most famously by Pinkydoll. The creator plays the role of a video-game NPC, responding to each gift type with a fixed, repeated line or gesture — "ice cream so good," "yes yes yes," "gang gang" — often for hours. The meta drove significant gift revenue, prompted widespread parody and think-pieces about gamified intimacy, and briefly crossed into mainstream US news coverage.

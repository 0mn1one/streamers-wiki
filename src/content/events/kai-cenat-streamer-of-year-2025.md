At the **2024 Streamer Awards** (held March 1, 2025 in Los Angeles, covering the 2024 calendar year), Kai Cenat won **Streamer of the Year** for the fourth consecutive time — a record in the award's short history. The win cemented Kai's position as the most-recognized creator of the post-2022 Twitch era, an equivalent placement to xQc's 2020–2022 dominance and Asmongold's mid-2010s WoW-era cultural centrality, compressed into a tighter window.

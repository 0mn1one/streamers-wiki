The **Streamer Awards** is an annual creator-community awards show founded and hosted by QTCinderella. The inaugural show ran in March 2022 in Los Angeles, streamed on Twitch, and drew a peak concurrent viewership of over 380,000 — one of the largest non-gaming Twitch events of that year. Subsequent editions have been produced in partnership with OTK and have become a tentpole event on the creator calendar.

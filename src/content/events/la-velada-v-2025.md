**La Velada del Año V** was staged at Estadi Olímpic Lluís Companys in Barcelona on July 26, 2025 — a larger venue than the 2024 edition's Bernabéu footprint — drawing approximately 70,000 in-venue attendees. The Twitch broadcast peaked at **approximately 4.1 million concurrent viewers**, setting a new all-time single-channel concurrent record on the platform. Musical performances included Quevedo, Bad Gyal, and Duki; the undercard featured several members of the 2024 KOI creator roster.

In **May 2025**, Plaqueboymax — a top-20 Twitch streamer through much of 2024 — signed a multi-year non-exclusive deal with Kick. Financial terms were not publicly disclosed. The signing followed a pattern of non-exclusive deals Kick had used since the 2023 xQc contract, and is representative of the 2024–2026 Kick strategy: use non-exclusivity as a wedge to pull creators onto the platform without forcing a zero-sum choice against Twitch.

On **September 10, 2024**, 100 Thieves announced that Valkyrae's ownership stake in the organization had been increased, formalizing a more senior partner role alongside founder/CEO Matthew "Nadeshot" Haag. Details of the stake change were not publicly disclosed, but the move is representative of a broader 2023–2025 trend in creator-led esports/lifestyle organizations structuring ownership to retain top talent as true partners rather than contractors.

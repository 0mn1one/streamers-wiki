## Overview

On **March 14, 2021**, Ludwig Ahgren started a subathon with a simple rule: each new Tier 1 subscription added 10 seconds to the timer. He expected the stream to last a few days. Instead it ran for **31 days**, hit a peak of **282,191 concurrent subs** (surpassing Ninja's previous Twitch record), and culminated in a scheduled April 13 end, at which point Ludwig capped the timer and ended the stream on his terms.

The subathon catapulted Ludwig from a mid-tier creator into a top-tier one, normalized the subathon format across the platform, and prefigured his January 2022 move to YouTube Live.

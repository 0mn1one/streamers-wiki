Rubén "Rubius" Doblas Gundersen is a Spanish-Norwegian creator who rose to prominence on YouTube in the early 2010s with gaming commentary and sketch content. His relocation from Spain to Andorra in 2021 for tax reasons — an episode that drew public commentary from Spanish officials — coincided with his shift in primary activity to Twitch live-streaming. He remains among the most-followed Spanish-language creators across both platforms.

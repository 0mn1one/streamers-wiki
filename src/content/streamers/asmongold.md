Zack "Asmongold" Hoyt has been one of the most-watched MMO streamers on Twitch since 2017. He began with classic World of Warcraft content and expanded into Final Fantasy XIV (2021 arc), Lost Ark, New World, Elden Ring, and broad Just Chatting content. He is a co-founder of OTK and remains one of the organization's most-watched members.

Asmongold's streaming style is intentionally low-production — single webcam, minimal graphics, long-form unfiltered commentary — and is often cited as a counterexample to the assumption that stream production budgets drive viewership.

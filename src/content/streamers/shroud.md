Michael "Shroud" Grzesiek is a former Cloud9 CS:GO professional who retired in 2018 to focus on streaming. His reputation rests on FPS mechanics — particularly his PUBG, Apex Legends and Valorant play — and he is regularly cited by other pros and streamers as among the most naturally gifted aimers of his generation. Shroud signed a Mixer exclusivity deal in 2019 and returned to Twitch after Mixer's 2020 shutdown.

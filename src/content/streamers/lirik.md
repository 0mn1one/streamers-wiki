Saqib "LIRIK" Zahid is one of the longest-tenured top-tier Twitch streamers, broadcasting continuously since 2011. His variety format — cycling rapidly through new releases across FPS, survival, RPG and GTA RP — is widely cited as foundational to the modern variety-streamer template. Consistently placed in the top 20 of monthly Twitch watch-time leaderboards through much of the 2010s and into the 2020s.

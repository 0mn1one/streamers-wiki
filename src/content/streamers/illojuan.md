Juan "IlloJuan" García Reyes is a Spanish streamer from Málaga whose career accelerated through GTA V roleplay servers, particularly the Spanish-language server Marbella Vice, which he co-founded. His broad variety format and distinctive Andalusian humor have made him a tentpole of Spanish Twitch culture.

Vikram "Vikkstar" Singh Barn is a founding member of the Sidemen. His solo channel is one of the longest-running Minecraft content channels on YouTube, later pivoting to Call of Duty: Warzone during its 2020–2022 peak. He also co-founded the esports organization XSET (since dissolved) in 2020.

Ironmouse is a Puerto Rican VTuber affiliated with VShojo. Her 2024 subathon briefly set the Twitch all-time active-subscription record (approx. 326,000 active subs) before being surpassed by Kai Cenat's Mafiathon 2 later that year. She is widely credited with bringing VTuber content to a broader English-speaking Twitch audience.

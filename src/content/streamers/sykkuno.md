Sykkuno is an American streamer who grew through the 2020 Among Us wave alongside Valkyrae, Corpse Husband and Disguised Toast. In May 2022 he signed a YouTube-exclusive deal, joining the second wave of YouTube Live's creator acquisitions. His style — quiet, collaborative, avoids conflict — is often cited as a counterpoint to the higher-adrenaline norms of top-tier streaming.

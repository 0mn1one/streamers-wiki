Nick "Nickmercs" Kolcheff is an American FPS streamer best known for Call of Duty: Warzone, MW3, and Apex Legends. A former FaZe Clan member and the leader of the MFAM community, he was one of Twitch's most-watched shooter streamers from 2019 to 2023 before moving his primary stream to Kick in 2024.

Tyler "Trainwreckstv" Niknam is an American streamer whose Twitch career centered on slots-gambling content through 2019–2022, making him one of the most-watched — and most controversial — figures in that vertical. He is a prominent early backer of Kick and has publicly advocated for the platform's creator-first revenue split. His move from Twitch to Kick in 2022–2023 was closely linked to Twitch's 2022 unauthorized-gambling restrictions.

Michael Reeves is an American software-and-hardware engineer and YouTube creator whose "cursed robotics" videos — build-driven tech-comedy content in which he creates deliberately hostile or absurd machines — helped establish the modern tech-humor creator format. He is a member of Offline TV and a public partner of LilyPichu.

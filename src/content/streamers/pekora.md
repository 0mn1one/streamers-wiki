Usada Pekora is a Japanese VTuber under Hololive Production's third-generation "Fantasy" branch, debuting in July 2019. She has repeatedly topped Hololive Japan's Super Chat revenue rankings and is the most recognizable figure from the Hololive Minecraft server's golden era. Her catchphrase "peko" appended to sentences became one of the most-referenced verbal tics in VTuber culture.

David "TheGrefg" Cánovas Martínez is a Spanish streamer best known for Fortnite content. On **January 11, 2021**, his stream revealing his in-game Fortnite Icon Series skin peaked at approximately **2.47 million concurrent viewers** on Twitch — then an all-time single-channel platform record, since surpassed by La Velada events. He remains one of the most-watched Spanish-language Twitch streamers and is a frequent collaborator with Ibai and AuronPlay.

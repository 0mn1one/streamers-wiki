Ibai Llanos Garatea is a Spanish streamer and the most-followed Spanish-language creator on Twitch. He co-founded the esports organization KOI with FC Barcelona footballer Gerard Piqué in 2022, and is the producer of two tentpole annual events that consistently set Twitch concurrent-viewership records:

- **Los Premios Ibai** — an annual streamer/creator awards show.
- **La Velada del Año** — an annual creator boxing event at stadiums across Spain. The 2024 edition (La Velada IV) peaked over 3.8 million concurrent viewers on Twitch, setting the platform's all-time concurrent record for a single-channel broadcast.

Jack "CouRage" Dunlop is an American streamer and 100 Thieves co-owner. Formerly an esports caster at MLG, he became a full-time streamer in 2018 and signed an exclusive YouTube Live deal in 2019 — one of the early marquee YouTube Live signings.

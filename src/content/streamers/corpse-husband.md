Corpse Husband is a faceless American creator who began with horror-narration YouTube videos in 2017. His breakout came in October 2020 when he joined the Valkyrae/Pokimane/Sykkuno Among Us lobbies, where his low-vocal-range voice became instantly recognizable. He crossed into music in 2020–2021 with several Billboard-charting tracks. Chronic health issues have limited his output in the 2020s but he retains one of the largest faceless-creator followings on YouTube.

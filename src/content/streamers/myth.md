Ali "Myth" Kabbani is an American streamer whose rise paralleled the 2018 Fortnite wave, where he served as a mainstay of TSM's competitive Fortnite roster. He transitioned to variety streaming during 2019–2020 and joined 100 Thieves in 2022.

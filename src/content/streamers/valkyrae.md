Rachell "Valkyrae" Hofstetter joined 100 Thieves in 2020 and became the face of YouTube Live's creator push during the 2020 Among Us wave, at one point holding the title of most-watched female streamer of the year on any platform. In January 2021 she was named a co-owner of 100 Thieves.

Hwang "Hanryang" In-ho is a South Korean streamer whose Twitch Korea audience transitioned with him to YouTube Live following the platform's February 2024 withdrawal from the country. He is among the most-followed Korean-language streamers on YouTube Live, representative of the post-2024 restructure of Korean streaming onto YouTube Live, SOOP, and Chzzk.

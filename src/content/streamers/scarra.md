William "Scarra" Li is a former League of Legends professional (Dignitas, Team Dignitas EU) turned Twitch streamer. In 2017 he co-founded **Offline TV** with Pokimane, Disguised Toast and LilyPichu — one of the earliest creator collective houses, which prefigured OTK, AMP and the broader 2020s house-content format.

Logan Paul is an American creator who rose to prominence on Vine and transitioned to YouTube vlogs in 2016. After a 2017 controversy in Japan that led to significant platform and advertiser consequences, he pivoted toward longer-form podcast content (Impaulsive), professional boxing, WWE wrestling, and the Prime Hydration beverage line he co-founded with KSI in 2022.

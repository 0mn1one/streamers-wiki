Tyson "TenZ" Ngo is a Canadian Valorant professional for Sentinels, winner of Valorant Masters Reykjavík 2021. He transitioned from CS:GO to Valorant at launch and quickly became the game's most recognizable pro, with a streaming audience that consistently ranks among the most-watched Valorant channels. His partner Kyedae is also a top streamer.

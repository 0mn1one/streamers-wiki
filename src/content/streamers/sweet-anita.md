Sweet Anita is a British Twitch streamer whose combination of Just Chatting content, long-form storytelling, and openness about living with Tourette syndrome have made her one of the UK's most-watched female creators. She has been widely covered in UK mainstream press and advocates for disability representation in creator spaces.

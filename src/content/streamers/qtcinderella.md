QTCinderella is an American Twitch streamer best known for cooking streams, Just Chatting content, and as the founder and host of the annual Streamer Awards, which she launched in 2022. In January 2023 she publicly addressed a high-profile deepfake incident involving multiple female creators — a moment widely credited with accelerating legal and platform-policy discussions around non-consensual AI imagery.

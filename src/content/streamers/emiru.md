Emily "Emiru" Schunk is an American Twitch streamer and cosplayer who joined OTK as one of its co-founders in 2021. Her content mixes cosplay, League of Legends, WoW, and in-person OTK productions. In 2024 she publicly disclosed being assaulted by a fan at TwitchCon, an incident that prompted an industry-wide conversation about creator-event safety.

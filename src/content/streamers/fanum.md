Roberto "Fanum" Gonzalez is an American streamer and co-founder of AMP. His on-stream running bit of **"Fanum taxing"** friends — taking food from Kai and other AMP members — crossed into broader Gen Z vocabulary through 2023 TikTok virality, the term becoming one of the most-used pieces of streamer-originated slang in the post-2020 cohort.

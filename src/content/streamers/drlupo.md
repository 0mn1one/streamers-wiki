Benjamin "DrLupo" Lupo is an American variety streamer best known for extensive charity work — he has raised tens of millions of dollars for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital across annual "Build Against Cancer" fundraising streams — and Escape from Tarkov content. In August 2021 he signed one of the earliest major exclusive deals with YouTube Live.

Simon "Miniminter" Minter is a founding member of the Sidemen and one of the most-followed British YouTube creators. FIFA gameplay commentary was his signature format through the 2010s; in the 2020s his output has centered around Sidemen group content, Sidemen Sundays, and co-running the Sidemen business portfolio.

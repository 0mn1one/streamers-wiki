Olajide "KSI" Olatunji is a British YouTuber, musician and professional boxer. A founding member of the Sidemen, he is also one of the most commercially successful individual creators in YouTube history, with platinum-certified music, the Misfits Boxing promotion, and Prime Hydration (co-founded with Logan Paul in 2022, which has generated well over a billion dollars in retail sales).

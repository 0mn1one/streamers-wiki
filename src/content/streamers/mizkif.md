Matthew "Mizkif" Rinaudo is an American Twitch streamer and co-founder of OTK. His content is primarily Just Chatting and elaborate in-person game shows and challenge streams filmed in the OTK Austin house. In 2022 he was temporarily removed from OTK's organizational roles during an internal dispute over the handling of allegations against a former streamer; he subsequently returned to the organization.
